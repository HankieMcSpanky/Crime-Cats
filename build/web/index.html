Early Development
▶ Play in Browser

Runs directly in your browser — no download needed

Play as mischievous Bengal cats on a mission to cause chaos around the house — knock things off tables, scratch the furniture, shred the toilet paper — all while avoiding the owner's watchful eye. Go solo or team up in local co-op for double the trouble.

🐈

Be the Cat

Walk, run, jump, and pounce on 4 legs. Swipe objects off shelves with your paw. Carry items in your mouth and drop them where they don't belong.

👁

Stealth Gameplay

The owner patrols the house with a vision cone. Stay out of sight, hide under furniture, and time your crimes carefully.

🏠

Destructible House

5 fully furnished rooms — living room, kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, and hallway. Every object is a potential target for feline mischief.

🎮

Local Co-op

Team up with a friend on the same keyboard. Player 1 uses WASD, Player 2 uses arrow keys. Coordinate your crimes for combo bonuses.

⭐

Score & Combos

Earn points for every crime committed. Chain crimes together for combo multipliers. How high can you score before getting caught?

💫

Suspicion System

The owner gets increasingly suspicious as crimes pile up. Push your luck — but get caught three times and it's game over.
